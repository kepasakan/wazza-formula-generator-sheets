Advanced Color Grading Techniques

⏱ 24 min · Lesson 6 of 12 · Section 2: Post-Production

In this lesson, we dive deep into professional color grading workflows used by top video editors and filmmakers. You'll learn how to use LUTs (Look-Up Tables), manipulate curves, and apply cinematic color profiles that will give your videos a polished, professional look.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to: apply and customize LUTs in your editor of choice, correct white balance issues in post, create consistent colour profiles across an entire project, and export color-accurate footage for different platforms.

💡 Pro Tip: Always color grade on a calibrated monitor. Your laptop screen may show colors differently than how your audience sees them.
